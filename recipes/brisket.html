Brisket

Description

Don't overcomplicate it, if you can maintain a steady temperature in your smoker, you can smoke a brisket

Ingredients

  • 1 whole packer brisket
  • 1/4 cup black pepper
  • 1.5 tbsp Lawry's seasoning salt
  • 1.5 tpsp kosher salt
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder

Steps

  1. Trim brisket. Easiest done directly from the refrigerator.
  2. Combine spice rub and apply liberally to brisket.
  3. Get smoker to 225-250F.
  4. Put brisket on smoker.
  5. When satisfied with bark development, wrap it in butcher paper. Or don't wrap it if you prefer.
  6. Start checking brisket for doneness @190F. Probe should slide in like butter.
  7. When done, rest brisket for at least 2 hours minimum.
